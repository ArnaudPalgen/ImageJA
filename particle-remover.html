Particle Remover

Author: Wayne Rasband (wsr at nih.gov)
History: 2000/09/07: first version
2002/01/31: erases particles to foreground color; bug fixes
2004/02/09: recompiled to support byte masks used in v1.32c and later
Requires: ImageJ 1.32c or later
Source: Particle_Remover.java
Installation: Download Particle_Remover.class and CustomAnalyzer.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: This is an extended ParticleAnalyzer that creates a copy of the image that has each included particle erased to the current foreground color. With stacks, it erases the particles in place and there is no undo.
