Twelve Bit Expander

Author: Wayne Rasband (wayne@codon.nih.gov)
Date: 2002/01/09
Source: Twelve_Bit_Expander.java
Installation: Download Twelve_Bit_Expander.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: This plugin unpacks a 16-bit little-endian image or stack that has two 12-bit pixels packed in three bytes.
