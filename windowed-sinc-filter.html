Windowed-Sinc Filter

Author: Kunito Yoshida (k.yoshida at imperial.ac.uk)
Department of Biological Sciences
Imperial College London
History: 2005/10/21: First version
Source: WindowedSinc_Filter.java
Installation: Download WindowedSinc_Filter.class to the plugins folder, or subfolder, and restart ImageJ.
Description: This plugin is a Java implementation of the windowed-sinc low-pass filter described in Chapter 16 of
"The Scientist and Engineer's Guide to Digital Signal Processing"
Steven W. Smith, Ph.D. California Technical Publishing
ISBN 0-9660176-3-3 (1997)
www.dspguide.com
www.dspguide.com/ch16.htm
It Performs low pass filtering of time series stacks along the time axis.
