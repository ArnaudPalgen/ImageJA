Line Analyzer

Author: Wayne Rasband (wayne@codon.nih.gov)
Date:2000/12/21
Limitations:Limited to one pixel wide straight lines
Source: Line_Analyzer.java
Installation: This plugin is built into ImageJ 1.31j or later.
Description: Calculates the mean, min, max and standard deviation of the pixel values along a straight line selection. Also displays the line length and angle.

The Analyze/Measure command in ImageJ 1.31j or later will measure mean, min, max or standard deviation of the pixels along a line selection if these parameters are checked in Analyze/Set Measurements.
