Mexican Hat Filter

Author: Dimiter Prodanov (dimiterpp at gmail.com)
History: 2012/11/10: First release
Source: Mexican_Hat_Filter.java
Installation: Drag and drop Mexican_Hat_Filter.class onto the "ImageJ" window.
Description: This plugin applies a Laplacian of Gaussian (Mexican Hat) filter to a 2D image.
