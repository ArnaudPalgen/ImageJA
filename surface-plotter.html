Surface Plotter

Author: Wayne Rasband (wsr at nih.gov)
History: 2000/10/03: First version
2002/03/19: Added "One Polygon Per Line" option
Limitations:Does not have the option to generate grayscale/color plots.
Source: Surface_Plotter.java
Installation: This plugin is built into ImageJ 1.28 and later as the Analyze/Surface Plot command.
Description: Generates 3D surface plots. Works best with small images or small selections. Smoothing the image may improve the quality of the plot.
See Also: 3D Surface Plot plugin by Kai Uwe Barthel
