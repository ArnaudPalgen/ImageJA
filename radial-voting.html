Iterative Radial Voting

Authors: Hang Chang (hchang at lbl.gov)
BahramParvin (b_parvin@lbl.gov)
History: 2009/7/28: First version
Source: Contained in ZIP archive
Installation: Download radialvoting_plugin_imagej.zip and extract the files. The instructions are in readme.pdf.
Description: This plug-in is provided by the Imaging and Informatics Lab of Life Sciences Division of Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (vision.lbl.gov). It is a "simplified version" of the method described in "Iterative Voting for Inference of Structural Saliency and Characterization of Subcellular Events," Bahram Parvin, Qing Yang, Ju Han, Hang Chang, Bjorn Rydbery and Mary Helen Barcellos-Hoff, in IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON IMAGE PROCESSING, 2007. In this simplified implementation, the Gaussian kernels are replaced with uniform kernels for improved computational performance. Please contact Hang Chang (hchang at lbl.gov) or BahramParvin (b_parvin at lbl.gov) with respect to the usage and further support of this package.
