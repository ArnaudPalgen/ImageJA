RGB Gray Merge

Authors: Eugene Tkachenko (etkachen@Dartmouth.EDU)
and Justin D. Pearlman
Date: 2001/12/12
Source: RGB_Gray_Merge.java
Installation: Download RGB_Gray_Merge to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: Combines up to four 8-bit grayscale images or stacks (gray, red, green and blue) into one RGB image or stack.
