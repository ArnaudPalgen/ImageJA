Fractal Surface Measurement

Author: Emerson Ferreira de Lucena (elucena.ferreira at terra.com.br)
History: 2010/02/06: Initial release
Source: Fractal_Surface_Measurement.java
Installation: Drag and drop Fractal_Surface_Measurement.class onto the "ImageJ" window (drag and drop installation requires ImageJ 1.43).
Description: This plugin implement the algorithm for direct measurement of the fractal dimension of fracture surface from height measurements obtained by projective covering method described in "Direct fractal measurement and multifractal properties of fracture surfaces", Heping Xie a, Jin-an Wang a, E. Stein, Physics Letters A 242 (1998) 41-50 (PDF).
