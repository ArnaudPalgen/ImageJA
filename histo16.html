16-bit Histogram

Author: Wayne Rasband (wayne@codon.nih.gov)
History: 2000/11/28: First version
2003/01/28: Uses the image or selection min and max; works correctly with signed images
Source: SixteenBit_Histogram.java
Installation: Copy SixteenBit_Histogram.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: Generates a tabular histogram of a 16-bit image or selection. Right click on the table to save it as a tab-delimited text file or to copy it to the clipboard.
