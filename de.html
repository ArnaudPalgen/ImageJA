Dual-Energy Algorithm

Author: Angelo Taibi (taibi at fe.infn.it)
University of Ferrara
Italy
History: 2003/06/26: First version
Source: DE_.java
Installation: Download DE_.java to the plugins folder, or subfolder, and compile and run using Plugins/Compile and Run. Restarting ImageJ will add a "DE" command to the Plugins menu or a submenu of the Plugins menu.
Description: This plugin implements the DUAL-ENERGY (DE) algorithm. It assumes that two 16-bit images, namely a LOW ENERGY (LE) image and a HIGH ENERGY (HE) image, are loaded before starting the DE analysis. For details on the dual-energy technique see "A. Taibi et al, Phys. Med. Biol. 48, 1945-1956, 2003" and references therein. This article is available online at www.iop.org/EJ/toc/0031-9155/48/13.

Acknowledgment: The DE_ plugin is based on the NORMALIZE_ plugin, written by Jeffrey Kuhn. It also makes use of the Panel_Window plugin, written by Wayne Rasband. The help of Francesco Sisini (francescosisini@yahoo.it) is also kindly acknowledged.
