X Shifter

Author: Peng Xi (xipeng1 at gmail.com)
Department of Chemistry, Michigan State University
History: 2007/05/03: First version
Source: X_Shifter.java
Installation: Download X_Shifter.class to the plugins folder, or subfolder, restart ImageJ, and there will be a new "X Shifter" command in the Plugins menu, or submenu.
Description: This plugin corrects the pixel mismatch of confocal spot scanning images or stacks. Choose a region that needs to be corrected, run the plugin, and input the amount of X shift. The program will shift the pixel amount on the region of interest.
See Also: Correct X Shift plugin
