Deinterlace

Author: Wayne Rasband (wsr at nih.gov)
History: 2002/05/16: First version
2007/09/18: Bug fix
Source: Deinterlace_.java
Installation: Copy Deinterlace_.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: This plugin de-interlaces an image by replacing the odd lines with the average of the adjacent even lines.
