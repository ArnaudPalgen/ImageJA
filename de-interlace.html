Deinterlace

Author: Wayne Rasband (wsr at nih.gov)
History: 2002/05/16: First version
2007/09/18: Bug fix
2011/07/12: Adds dialog with three choices
Source: Deinterlace_.java
Installation: Drag and drop Deinterlace_.class to the "ImageJ" window.
Description: This plugin deinterlaces an image or stack using one of three methods:
  • Replace odd lines with the average of the adjacent lines.
  • Replace even lines with the average of the adjacent lines.
  • Do both of the above and double the number of frames.
