PDS Reader

Author: Mike Martin (tahoemike@earthlink.net)
Date: 2001/10/01
Source: PDS_Reader.java
Installation: Download PDS_Reader.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: This plugin opens and displays most uncompressed Planetary Data System (PDS) image files. A sample image (pds.img.zip) is available.
