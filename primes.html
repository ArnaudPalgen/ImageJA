Primes

Author: Werner Bailer (werner@wbailer.com)
Date:2000/04/25
Requirements:ImageJ 1.15z or later
Source: Primes_.java
Installation: Download Primes_.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: This plugin finds all prime numbers less than 32,000 and displays them in a window. The prime values are displayed in the status bar as the cursor moves over the window.
